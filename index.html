What is PropertyGum ?

  • UK Properties For Sale Reviewed By Users

    PropertyGum lets crowd analyse & write reviews about properties listed for sale on property portals like RightMove & Zoopla. Buyers get immediate insights about listed property prices, acceptable price range & peoples opinion about property market.

  • Track Property Price Changes Made By Estate Agents

    It also tracks property price changes made by estate agents (through chrome browser extension) which provides useful information when negotiating or checking up property for buying.

  • Property Reviews By Users

    Read & write reviews about property for sale listings at rightmove. Rate or vote for them or critique listing prices.

  • Find Best Property Deals

    Discover properties for sale at below market prices. Check community opinions and get insights about current market trends.

How PropertyGum works ?

  • 1. Install our free chrome extension
  • 2. Browse RightMove (Zoopla coming soon) website and we'll show PropertyGum community ratings on property listing page.
  • 3. Become part of community by posting your views, rating and voting for best deal properties for sale.
